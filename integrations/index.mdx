HappyCapy can connect to your commonly used platforms to make your work more efficient. Currently, GitHub and Notion integrations are supported with simple Token-based configuration.

<Note>
**What is a Token?** A Token is like a key you give HappyCapy so that it can access other platforms on your behalf. The setup process is simple and secure.
</Note>
