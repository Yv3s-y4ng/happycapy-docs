## What It Looks Like

In normal chat mode, QQ messages go straight to Claude and replies come back in QQ. In emotional companion mode, the bot takes on a character with its own personality and speaking style. Both modes support AI-generated images and short videos right in the chat.

## Setup Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Register a QQ Bot">
    Go to [QQ Open Platform](https://q.qq.com/qqbot/openclaw/index.html) and scan the QR code with QQ on your phone to register a developer account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a Bot Application">
    Click "Create Bot" on the management page. Give it a name and avatar.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Get Credentials and Take a Screenshot">
    Find your **AppID** and **AppSecret** on the bot management page, then take a screenshot of that page.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Deploy in Happycapy">
    Open a new Happycapy conversation and send the following prompt:

    ```
    安装这个 https://github.com/Yv3s-y4ng/qq-bridge 到本地并部署
    ```

    Then send your credentials screenshot to Happycapy. It will automatically extract the AppID and AppSecret and complete the deployment.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once deployed, your QQ bot is live.

## Two Modes

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Normal Chat Mode" icon="comments">
    QQ messages are forwarded directly to Claude, and Claude's replies are sent back to QQ. It's like using Happycapy right inside QQ.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Emotional Companion Mode" icon="heart">
    Built-in AI characters (3 female + 3 male) with unique personalities, speech patterns, and emotional arcs. Supports AI-generated images and videos.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Commands

| Command | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| `/mode` | Switch between normal chat and companion mode |
| `/reset` | Reset all state and re-select mode |
