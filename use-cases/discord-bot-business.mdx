## Background

**User**: gffi (from the happycapy WeChat community)

gffi runs a brick-and-mortar chain business with 20 years of experience. Despite having no prior programming background, he has been using happycapy to build technical solutions for both personal projects and his company.

> "You provide the world, users are the specific characters in it."
> — gffi

## What He Built

### 1. A 24/7 Discord Bot

gffi built a fully functional Discord bot that runs continuously on happycapy's sandbox environment. The bot features AI-powered conversations, image analysis, and persistent memory across sandbox restarts.

He solved real engineering challenges: process management with Supervisord, custom watchdog monitoring, Git-based auto-backup every 30 minutes, and conversation history compression.

### 2. Website Deployment via Cloudflare

gffi also built and deployed a sharing website. His role? Just registering the domain name. Everything else — DNS configuration, Cloudflare Pages deployment, and all the technical operations — was handled entirely by happycapy.

> "I only registered the domain name. All the DNS operations and processes were handled by happycapy."

### 3. Migrating an Entire Business

The most impressive part: gffi is now moving his entire chain business operations onto happycapy. He's already seen significant cost savings for one of his projects.

> "It's not about API call costs — it's that the whole logic works."
> "I've been in chain businesses for 20 years, so this should be a valuable sharing from a real player."
