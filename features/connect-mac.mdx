## What It Is

Connect My Mac lets Happycapy run tasks directly on your Mac. Instead of working only inside the cloud sandbox, Happycapy can operate on your local machine — managing files, running scripts, and controlling applications.

You can connect multiple Macs at the same time.

## How to Connect

1. Open **Settings** (click your avatar in the bottom-left corner)
2. Go to **Connect My Mac**
3. Click **+ Connect a Mac** and follow the setup instructions

Once connected, your Mac appears in the Devices list. Happycapy can now run tasks on it directly.

## What You Can Do

With a connected Mac, Happycapy can:

- Run commands and scripts on your local machine
- Access and manage local files
- Control local applications
- Work across both the cloud sandbox and your Mac in the same session

## Multiple Devices

Connect more than one Mac to let Happycapy work across machines. Each device shows up separately in Settings, and you can disconnect any device at any time.
