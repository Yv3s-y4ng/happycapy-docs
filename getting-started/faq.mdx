## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="shield-check" title="Is my data safe? Will privacy be compromised?">
    Your privacy and data security are our primary concerns. Here's how we protect you:

    ### Local priority
    Your files only reach the cloud sandbox environment when you actively upload them. They won't affect your local system.

    ### Sandbox isolation
    All code runs in an independent sandbox environment, completely isolated from your main system to ensure security.

    ### Transparent and controllable
    Every step of AI operation is clearly visible. You can view, pause, or modify operations at any time.

    ### Autonomous authority
    When connecting to third-party services (GitHub, Notion, etc.), all permissions are authorized by you and can be revoked at any time.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="file-arrow-up" title="How large of a file can happycapy handle?">
    happycapy can handle files of various sizes, but we recommend these best practices:

    ### Small files (< 50MB)
    Upload directly to the conversation box for instant processing.

    ### Large files (> 50MB)
    For better performance:
    1. Upload to GitHub first
    2. Let happycapy download from the cloud

    This approach is faster and more stable for large files.

    <Note>
      **GitHub integration tip**: Generate a GitHub Token and happycapy can help you download, upload, and manage files. See our GitHub integration guide for detailed instructions.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="clock" title="How long do files stay in my workspace?">
    Files in your happycapy workspace are persistent:

    - **Uploaded files**: Remain in `uploads/` until you delete them
    - **Generated outputs**: Stay in `outputs/` until you delete them
    - **Desktop sessions**: Saved automatically and accessible anytime

    You have full control over your files and can delete them whenever you want.
