## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="shield-check" title="Is my data safe? Will privacy be compromised?">
    We take your privacy and data security seriously. Here's how we protect you:

    ### Local priority
    Your files only reach the cloud sandbox environment when you actively upload them. They won't affect your local system.

    ### Sandbox isolation
    All code runs in an independent sandbox environment, isolated from your main system.

    ### Transparent and controllable
    Every step of AI operation is clearly visible. You can view, pause, or modify operations at any time.

    ### Autonomous authority
    When connecting to third-party services (GitHub, Notion, etc.), all permissions are authorized by you and can be revoked at any time.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="brain" title="What AI models does Happycapy support?">
    Happycapy routes to multiple AI providers through its gateway:

    - **Claude** — Opus 4.6, Sonnet 4.6, Haiku 4.5
    - **Google** — Gemini 3 Pro Preview, Gemini 3 Pro Image
    - **xAI** — Grok 3
    - **MiniMax** — M2.7

    <Tip>
      The model list updates as we add new providers. You don't need to manage API keys — the gateway handles routing automatically.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="file-arrow-up" title="How large of a file can Happycapy handle?">
    Happycapy can handle files of various sizes, but we recommend these best practices:

    ### Small files (< 50MB)
    Upload directly to the conversation box for instant processing.

    ### Large files (> 50MB)
    For better performance:
    1. Upload to GitHub first
    2. Let Happycapy download from the cloud

    This approach is faster and more stable for large files.

  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="dollar-sign" title="What are the pricing plans?">
    We offer flexible plans for different needs:

    ### Free tier
    Get started with limited monthly usage to explore Happycapy's capabilities.

    ### Pro plan
    Generous token limits for regular users who want more capacity.

    ### Max plan
    **Significantly more tokens than Pro** — perfect for heavy users, creators, and professionals who want to focus on their work, not their usage.

    Visit [happycapy.ai/pricing](https://happycapy.ai/pricing) for current pricing details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="globe" title="Can I use Happycapy on mobile devices?">
    Yes! Happycapy runs entirely in your browser, so you can access it from:

    - Desktop computers (Windows, Mac, Linux)
    - Tablets (iPad, Android tablets)
    - Smartphones (iOS, Android)

    The interface adapts to your screen size, though some complex tasks are easier on larger screens.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="language" title="What languages does Happycapy support?">
    Happycapy supports multiple languages for conversation:

    - English
    - Chinese (Simplified and Traditional)
    - Japanese
    - Korean
    - Spanish
    - French
    - German
    - And more

    Simply talk to Happycapy in your preferred language, and it will respond accordingly.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="plug" title="Can I integrate Happycapy with other tools?">
    Happycapy runs on Claude Code, so it supports most MCP servers, APIs, and CLI tools that work with Claude Code. Common integrations include:

    - **GitHub**: Repository management, file uploads, version control
    - **Notion**: Document management and collaboration
    - **Gmail**: Email processing and automation
    - **Google Drive**: Cloud storage integration

    All integrations require your authorization. You can set them up by asking Happycapy in a conversation.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="code" title="Do I need coding knowledge to use Happycapy?">
    **No coding knowledge required!**

    Happycapy is designed for everyone, regardless of technical background. You communicate with Happycapy in natural language, just like chatting with a friend.

    However, if you do have coding knowledge, you can:
    - Give more technical instructions
    - Request specific implementations
    - Review and modify generated code

    Happycapy adapts to your expertise level.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="clock" title="How long do files stay in my workspace?">
    Files in your Happycapy workspace are persistent:

    - **Uploaded files**: Remain in `uploads/` until you delete them
    - **Generated outputs**: Stay in `outputs/` until you delete them
    - **Desktop sessions**: Saved automatically and accessible anytime

    You have full control over your files and can delete them whenever you want.
  </Accordion>

    - **Documentation**: Browse our comprehensive guides
    - **Community**: Join our Discord community for peer support
    - **Email support**: Contact support@happycapy.ai
    - **In-app help**: Click the help button for contextual assistance

    For Max plan users, we offer priority support with faster response times.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
